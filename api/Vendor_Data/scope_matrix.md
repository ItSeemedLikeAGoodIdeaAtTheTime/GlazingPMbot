# Glazing Contractor Scope Matrix

## Purpose
Categorize contract requirements into standard scope types, then match to vendors.

---

## Scope Categories

### 1. STOREFRONT
**Description**: Aluminum-framed fixed and operable glazing systems, typically 1-2 stories

**Typical Materials Needed**:
- Aluminum framing system (Kawneer 451, similar)
- Monolithic or IG glass units
- Door hardware (closers, panic devices, locks)
- Perimeter sealants
- Setting blocks and gaskets
- Anchors and fasteners

**Typical Vendors**:
- Framing: Kawneer, Oldcastle
- Glass: Vitrum, Hartung, Tacoma Glass
- Hardware: IML, Mayflower
- Sealants: Colorado Steel & Sash, Atlas Supply

**Lead Time**: 8-12 weeks (metal), 4-8 weeks (glass)

**Spec Sections**: 084113, 088000

---

### 2. CURTAIN WALL
**Description**: Engineered aluminum and glass wall systems, multi-story

**Typical Materials Needed**:
- Curtain wall system (stick-built or unitized)
- Insulated glass units (high-performance)
- Structural silicone sealant
- Perimeter sealants
- Anchors and embedments
- Thermal breaks

**Typical Vendors**:
- Systems: Kawneer, Oldcastle (high-end)
- Glass: Vitrum (premium), Hartung
- Sealants: Colorado Steel & Sash (structural)
- Metal fab: North Clad, ABF (if custom panels)

**Lead Time**: 12-16 weeks (system), 6-8 weeks (glass)

**Spec Sections**: 084300, 088000

---

### 3. MONOLITHIC GLASS & FRAMES BY OTHERS
**Description**: Glass only supply, frames provided/installed by others (GC, millwork, etc.)

**Typical Materials Needed**:
- Monolithic glass (tempered, laminated, or annealed)
- Edge treatments (polished, seamed)
- Setting materials (if we install)
- Sealants (if we install)

**Typical Vendors**:
- Glass: Tacoma Glass (fast), Vitrum (high-end), Hartung
- Sealants: Colorado Steel & Sash

**Lead Time**: 2-3 days (Tacoma), 2-4 weeks (others)

**Spec Sections**: 088000

---

### 4. FIRE-RATED GLAZING
**Description**: Fire-rated glass and framing systems (doors, borrowed lights, walls)

**Typical Materials Needed**:
- Fire-rated glass (20-120 min ratings)
- Fire-rated frames (if not by others)
- Fire-rated hardware (listed assemblies)
- Fire-rated sealants
- Labels and certification

**Typical Vendors**:
- Glass: TGP/Allegiant (PRIMARY - often only option)
- Hardware: IML (fire-rated assemblies)
- Sealants: Atlas Supply (fire-rated)

**Lead Time**: 16+ weeks (TGP/Allegiant)

**Spec Sections**: 081416 (fire-rated doors), 088000, 088313

**CRITICAL**: Fire-rated is specialty - often single-source (TGP)

---

### 5. INTERIOR GLAZING
**Description**: Interior glass walls, partitions, sidelites, borrowed lights

**Typical Materials Needed**:
- Tempered glass (safety glazing required)
- Aluminum or steel framing
- Sliding/swinging hardware
- Sealants and gaskets

**Typical Vendors**:
- Glass: Tacoma Glass (fast turn), Hartung
- Framing: Kawneer, Oldcastle
- Hardware: CRL, IML

**Lead Time**: 2-4 weeks

**Spec Sections**: 088000, 102813 (interior glass walls)

---

### 6. MIRRORS
**Description**: Wall mirrors, typically for dance studios, gyms, retail

**Typical Materials Needed**:
- Mirror glass (1/4" typical)
- Mirror mastic adhesive
- J-channel or clips
- Safety backing film (optional)

**Typical Vendors**:
- Mirrors: Tacoma Glass, Hartung
- Adhesive/clips: Tacoma Screw

**Lead Time**: 1-2 weeks

**Spec Sections**: 088300

---

### 7. ENTRANCE DOORS & HARDWARE
**Description**: All-glass doors, aluminum-framed doors, door hardware

**Typical Materials Needed**:
- Door glass (tempered)
- Door frames (if aluminum)
- Panic hardware, closers, pivots, locks
- Thresholds and weatherstripping

**Typical Vendors**:
- Glass: Tacoma Glass, Vitrum
- Hardware: IML (PRIMARY), Mayflower
- All-glass hardware: FHC, CRL

**Lead Time**: 8 weeks (hardware), 2-4 weeks (glass)

**Spec Sections**: 081113 (hollow metal), 083613 (all-glass), 087100 (hardware)

---

### 8. SPECIALTY GLASS
**Description**: Lead glass (x-ray), bullet-resistant, sound control, decorative

**Typical Materials Needed**:
- Specialty glass products
- Specialty framing (if required)
- Specialty installation requirements

**Typical Vendors**:
- Lead glass: (Need to identify - may be specialty supplier)
- Decorative: GlassPro (high-end)
- Bullet-resistant: (Need to identify)

**Lead Time**: Varies widely, 12-20 weeks typical

**Spec Sections**: Varies by application

---

### 9. METAL PANELS (Adjacent Scope)
**Description**: Metal panels adjacent to glazing (spandrel covers, column covers, etc.)

**Typical Materials Needed**:
- Aluminum or steel panels
- Paint/finish
- Attachment systems
- Sealants

**Typical Vendors**:
- Fabrication: North Clad, ABF
- Paint: New Finishes, Accurate Industries
- Raw material: Coast Aluminum

**Lead Time**: 12 weeks

**Spec Sections**: 074213 (metal wall panels)

---

### 10. RAILING SYSTEMS WITH GLASS
**Description**: Glass railing infill panels

**Typical Materials Needed**:
- Tempered laminated glass (safety)
- Railing frames (usually by others)
- Standoffs or base shoe
- Sealants

**Typical Vendors**:
- Glass: Vitrum (high-quality), Hartung
- Hardware: CRL, FHC

**Lead Time**: 4-6 weeks

**Spec Sections**: 057313 (glass railing)
